About

Hiyah! I'm Mariah and I'm a data scientist.

I’m an ambitious Question Master who likes numbers and programming.

After being named Math student of the Year, I started learning to program at UNC Chapel Hill (Go Heels!) in 2013. Python became and still is the language I’m most proficient in and its come in handy as I’ve recently started my journey to becoming a data scientist.
